Privacy Policy

Personal Information we receive and collect

Each time you visit our Website, we collect some anonymized information about you such as:

  • The region from which you navigate the web page
  • Your browser and device type, as well as the access time and the web page, which you came from
  • Your activity on our Website (e.g. session duration, number of pages viewed)

We value the privacy of our clients and store only the minimum required personal information. Upon subscription, we collect following information (without this data, ZuriAnalytics cannot offer the Services):

  • a token (an alphanumeric string) assigned upon registration
  • email address

How we use your data?

ZuriAnalytics uses Website visitors' data for the following general purposes:

  1. To keep track of the number of visits to the Website and the Services, as well as the behavior of the Website visitors, with a view to introducing improvements and improving usability of them
  2. To better understand the needs of users in the aggregate, diagnose problems, analyze trends, and better understand and market to clients and users
  3. To identify users with similar behavior as these of the Website visitors', as well as retarget Website visitors

    4. Additionally, we use data provided by clients of ZuriAnalytics for following purposes:

  4. To allow security and authentication
  5. To enable us to operate the Services and provide them to the clients
  6. To contact clients about their account and provide service support
  7. To keep clients informed about the Services, features, surveys, offers, and events we think they might find useful
  8. To sell or market ZuriAnalytics Products and Services
  9. To use aggregate (non-identifiable) statistics about the clients in order to approach similar audiences and market the Services
  10. To share aggregate (non-identifiable) statistics about users of the Services to prospective partners

How do we store your data?

The collected Personal Information is stored safely on our servers based in Germany and is being processed cautiously by ZuriAnalytics employees based in Switzerland. ZuriAnalytics will keep clients' Personal Information for the length of the Service contract plus up to 24 months. Once this period has expired, we will delete your data by anonymizing your records on our servers.

By visiting ZuriAnalytics or submitting any Personal Information via the Services, you consent to the collection, transfer, storage, and use of your Personal Information in the manner set out above. If you do not consent to the use of your Personal Information in these ways, please stop using the Services.

Privacy policy for cookies

By visiting ZuriAnalytics or using the Services, you agree to the use of cookies in your browser. Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our Websites, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies. Cookies remain stored at the end of a browser session and can be called up again when the site is visited again. In order to avoid this, please delete your cookies or set your Internet browser to not store cookies. On our Website, we use functionality and advertising cookies. You can find further information here.

Privacy policy for Facebook

Our website uses features from Facebook Inc. When you access our pages with Facebook plug-ins, a connection is established between your browser and the Facebook servers. Data is already being transferred to Facebook in the process. If you have a Facebook account, this data can be linked to it. If you do not want this data to be linked to your Facebook account, please log out of Facebook before visiting our site. You can learn more here.

Privacy policy for Google Tag Manager

Our Website uses Google Tag Manager, especially in relation to the use of Google Analytics, which is a web analytics service provided by Google Inc. To disable Google Analytics, Google provides a browser plug-in here. Google Analytics uses cookies. The information collected by the cookie about the use of our website (including your IP address) is usually transferred to a Google server in the USA and stored there. Google will use this information to evaluate your use of the Website, compiling reports on Website activity and providing other services related to Website activity and internet usage. Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Google's behalf. You can learn more here.
